Blackshot ERA (Primary / Secondary Education)

One of the first multiplayer FPS games I've ever played. It was fun not because the game was good (P2W FPS Games suck.), but due to the interactions with other fellow players.

Many became addicted to Team Fortress 2, an FPS Game developed by Valve. It was fun and had many classes to choose from, my favourite being Sniper, which I clocked 1000 + hours into. Nothing lasts forever, and Games ain't an exception. TF2 players moved to CS:GO because it was a "More competitive game". CS:GO was a paid game back then; as an individual who frown upon paid games, I did not pick CS:GO up.
